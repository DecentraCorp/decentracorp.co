The World's first Decentralized Autonomous Corporation

DAC

(Decentralized Autonomous Corporation)

DecentraCorp is the worlds first Decentralized Autonomous Corporation. Anyone can become a DecentraCorp Member and begin producing products powered by the CryptoPatent Blockchain. DecentraCorp will empower the individual to change the world with open source Ideas capable of mining NotioCoin!

DecentraCorp seeks to redefine how a global corporation operates by giving every member an equal say in how the corporation is run. DecentraCorp is a corporation built by the people for the betterment of humanity.

Blockchain powered Smart-Contracts allow for a new type of corporation; one that is completely transparent and accountable to both its members and the general public. Every action DecentraCorp takes is trustlessly stored for the world to see on DecentraCorps public blockchain. With no imposed top down structure or hierarchy, every DecentraCorp Member is the companies CEO with an equal say in how the Corporation operates. Together we will build a better future through open and free cooperation built around the ideals of Open-Source and spontaneous order!

We are currently under development and in our beta testing phase. So come join us and make DecentraCorp even better.

Announcements

September 2019

  • 2019/02/15 All DecentraCorp projects have been updated to run on ZeppelinOS allowing us to perform on-chain updates to all smart-contracts.With this advancement the time frame for our beta launch has been moved forward by several months!
  • 2019/03/10 The DecentraCorp Proof of Authority Network and the CryptoPatent Blockchain are now running globally with the staking of the second ChaosMiner by our core Dev-Team member Philip in China! We Are working hard to redisign Our dApp's to work under a new, web2 landing page model and plan the official beta launch for june.
  • 2019/05/13 We are proud to announce that the beta versions for DecentraCorp, the CryptoPatent Blockchain and the ChaosCasino are all up and running! The launch of the Depot has been postponed as it is undergoing a more radical redesign on its back end that will allow it to act as a marketplace for digital goods as well as physical ones.
  • 2019/05/26 Our tutorial section has now been updated with instructions for connecting Metamask Directly to a PoA bootnode. This eliminates the need to run a local node when interacting with DecentraCorps Proof of Authority Network!
  • 2019/06/15 We Accidently Invented an alternative method for account management! This new method of account management called the Universal Wallet and it allows for an ethereum account to safely be stored on IPFS and linked to a users credentials(I.e USERNAME/PASSWORD) through a smart-contract called the Account Abstraction Contract. The Universal Wallet is planned to be an Open-Source account management tool for dApp builders on EVM based Networks. More Details coming soon!
  • 2019/06/29 The Universal Wallet has entered an early development phase! We have successfully built a working proto-type and implemented it into both the DecentraCorp Members Portal dApp AND the ChaosCasino. At the moment the Universal Wallet relies on an http(not https) connection to the DecentraCorp Boot Node running on AWS. Due to this it is HIGHLY RECOMMENDED that if you decide to participate in our beta release test network that you DO NOT use your real email & password. Any two word combination will work as long as its not already in use. Just make sure that you remeber your fake credentials as you will not be able to recover your accoount without them!
  • 2019/07/06 We have finished up An early draft of the Universal Wallet WhitePaper explaining in detail how it and the Account Abstraction Contract work together to safely encrypt and store user accounts. We are still working hard on solving the http security issue and are currently exploring the use of IFrames to silo UW account information from the dApp it is implemented in.
  • 2019/07/27 We are currently exploring a new venture into the DeFi space known as DecentraStocks! Using DecentraStocks, Decentracorp will seek stabalize the Price of NotioCoin by allowing DecentraStock holders to stake their stocks for NotioCoin by locking them into a Collateralized Debt position worth a percentage of the stocks value. More info and updates to the DecentraCorp WhitePaper coming soon!
