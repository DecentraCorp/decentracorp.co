the Depot is a Decentralized Market-Place capable of offering a Discount on any item sold through it if that item is purchased with NotioCoin. This is made possible by the Depot being able to mint the difference between an items asking price and its discounted price through the DecentraCorp Contract. The Depot is undergoing a major upgrade that will allow it to act as a Market-Place for both Physical AND digital goods like ERC721 collectable tokens and other CryptoCurrencies.
